We’re Building the Future of Carbon Tracking

NetZeroX is an AI-powered carbon footprint tracker that helps individuals and businesses monitor and reduce their CO₂ emissions in real time. By combining IoT sensors and AI-driven analytics, we provide accurate insights into environmental impact, making sustainability effortless and actionable. Our solution is cost-effective, real-time, and data-driven, utilizing ESP-01 and Arduino Nano to collect sensor data on air quality, temperature, and human activity. Unlike traditional carbon tracking methods, NetZeroX offers automation and AI-based predictions, enabling smarter decision-making for a greener planet. We collaborate with businesses and individuals to simplify carbon footprint reduction, making it easier to transition to eco-friendly practices without sacrificing efficiency or affordability. With NetZeroX, sustainability is no longer a challenge—it’s a smart choice.
